About The Project

In 2021, my fellow classmates and I were assigned a task for the Software Project Management course. We were tasked with directly engaging with the community, particularly Small and Medium Enterprises (UMKM), to assist them in digitalizing their products. Our chosen client was Warung Sate Tegal Pa Win, located in Depok, West Java.

We created a website for Warung Sate Tegal Pa Win so that their products could be easily ordered through the website and customers could enjoy free delivery within specific kilometer criteria. In this project, I served as the UI/UX Designer.
